Exclusively Behind The Scenes

Who we are

We are a social club with a purpose. Part of our mission is to help provide necessary funding to address the homeless epidemic that is growing in our local communities. Let us explain. Exclusively Behind The Scenes invites you to join our club and by doing so, participate or help support us in the many events that we have to offer. All the while, Exclusively Behind The Scenes issues a percentage of raised funds as donations to popular well known organizations that provides much needed resources & help to alleviate homelessness for all our communities. We want to make a difference.
